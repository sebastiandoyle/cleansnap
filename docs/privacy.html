Privacy Policy

CleanSnap - Photo Cleanup App

Effective Date: December 23, 2024
Last Updated: December 23, 2024

Introduction

CleanSnap ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Key Point: CleanSnap operates entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

CleanSnap is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do NOT collect:

  • Your photos, videos, or any media files
  • Your contacts or address book information
  • Your location data
  • Device identifiers or tracking information
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Any personally identifiable information

How the App Works

CleanSnap performs all operations locally on your device:

  • Photo Analysis: Photos are analyzed on your device to detect duplicates. No images are ever uploaded or transmitted.
  • Contact Merging: Contact analysis happens entirely on your device. Your contacts are never shared.
  • Secret Vault: Photos in the vault are stored locally on your device with PIN protection. We cannot access your vault.
  • Storage Analysis: Storage calculations are performed locally without any data transmission.

Permissions We Request

CleanSnap requests the following permissions to function:

  • Photo Library Access: Required to scan and identify duplicate photos. Your photos never leave your device.
  • Contacts Access: Required to find and merge duplicate contacts. Your contacts are processed locally only.

Third-Party Services

CleanSnap uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and in-app purchases. Apple's privacy policy applies to these transactions.
  • StoreKit: For subscription management. Purchase data is handled by Apple according to their privacy policy.

In-App Purchases

CleanSnap offers premium subscriptions through the Apple App Store. All payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, your information is protected by your device's built-in security features, including:

  • Device encryption
  • Face ID / Touch ID protection
  • Passcode protection

Children's Privacy

CleanSnap does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is designed as a general utility and does not target children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. All your information remains under your control on your device.

Consent
