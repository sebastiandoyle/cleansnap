Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find duplicate photos?

Open CleanSnap and tap on "Duplicates" from the home screen or bottom tab. The app will automatically scan your photo library and group identical photos together. You can then select which copies to delete.

Will CleanSnap delete my photos without asking?

No. CleanSnap always shows you a review screen before any deletion. You have full control over which photos to keep or remove. We recommend keeping at least one copy of each photo.

Can I recover deleted photos?

Deleted photos go to your iPhone's "Recently Deleted" album in the Photos app, where they remain for 30 days. You can recover them from there if needed.

How does the Secret Vault work?

The Secret Vault lets you hide private photos behind a 4-digit PIN. Photos in the vault are stored securely on your device and are not visible in your regular photo library.

I forgot my vault PIN. What do I do?

For security reasons, we cannot recover your vault PIN. If you forget it, you'll need to delete and reinstall the app, which will remove the vault contents.

Does CleanSnap upload my photos?

No. CleanSnap works entirely offline on your device. Your photos are never uploaded to any server. Your privacy is our priority.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions > CleanSnap > Cancel Subscription. Your premium access will continue until the end of the current billing period.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Go to Settings in CleanSnap and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.
