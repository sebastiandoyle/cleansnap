Delete Screenshots on iPhone: The Fast Way

Screenshots pile up fast. That quick capture of an address, a funny meme, or a confirmation code - they all add up. Most iPhone users have 500-2000 screenshots taking up 2-8GB of storage.

Find Your Screenshots

iOS automatically groups screenshots. Here's how to find them:

  1. Open Photos app
  2. Tap Albums
  3. Scroll to Media Types
  4. Tap Screenshots

Delete All Screenshots (Manual Method)

  1. In Screenshots album, tap Select
  2. Tap Select All (top left)
  3. Tap trash icon
  4. Confirm deletion
  5. Important: Empty Recently Deleted folder too!

How Many Screenshots Do You Have?

Check Settings > General > iPhone Storage > Photos to see how much space media is using. Screenshots are often 1-3MB each, so 1000 screenshots = 1-3GB of storage!
