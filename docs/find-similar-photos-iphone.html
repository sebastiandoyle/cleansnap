Find Similar Photos on iPhone: Complete Guide

Similar photos are different from exact duplicates - they're those 15 shots you took trying to get the perfect selfie, or burst mode captures. They can use even MORE storage than duplicates.

What Are Similar Photos?

  • Burst shots - Multiple frames from burst mode
  • Near-duplicates - Same scene, slightly different angle
  • Photo series - Multiple attempts at the same shot
  • Live Photo frames - The video portion of Live Photos

The Problem With Similar Photos

Unlike exact duplicates, iOS doesn't have a built-in way to find similar photos. You'd have to:

  1. Scroll through every photo manually
  2. Remember which ones look alike
  3. Decide which to keep
  4. Delete one by one

For a library of 5,000+ photos, this could take 10+ hours.
